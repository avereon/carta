Getting Started

Welcome, and thank you for using Cartesia. Cartesia is a computer aided design (CAD) mod for Xenon. Designed with simplicity and user experience in mind, Cartesia offers a large set of simple commands to construct your design. Cartesia provides common geometry, modifiers, snaps and transforms as well as unlimited layers. Bring your vision to life with Cartesia.
